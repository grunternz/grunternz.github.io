13ADE: Computer Art Design, Entire Year, 2009

Art Design Portfolio

A look back at the final product produced in my second to last year at high school.

Process

For the best part of a year we were tasked with designing and creating something substantial. We all were required to design a brand, after which we were to expand upon it and create some form of information medium around it, such as a magazine in my case. Our final work was to be printed and displayed on a 3-fold A1 cardboard folder. After the completion of the course, all folders were sent to be alongside work from other schools throughout the nation, where they were all marked against each other. It was important to show our thought and design process throughout the folder; producing a good end result wasn't enough on it's own. I am very proud of my result; the hard work and late nights paid off in the end as I achieved Excellence, which was only given to the top 5% in 2009. I have clearly changed and or evolved as a person since then which is no surprise, but all I see now are things I would've done differently. I'm my own worst critic.
